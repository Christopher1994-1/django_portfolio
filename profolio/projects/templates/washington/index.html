Washington

Experience the darker side of Washington state and the greater Pacific Northwest.

Washington state is a place that captivates people's hearts with its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and prosperous economy. However, there is an unspoken allure to the state that many overlook: the beauty of its grey skies and melancholic ambiance. While some may shy away from the dark and eerie elements of Washington, others appreciate the ethereal charm that it possesses. There is something enchanting about the creepy scenery that the state has to offer, and it beckons those who seek to explore its shadowy corners. It is this paradoxical blend of light and darkness that makes Washington state truly captivating and unique.





Shoreline

Washington State has a beautiful shoreline showcases the pacific ocean. But during certain times of the year, some months even the beaches are empty, grey and eerie with mist over the horizon.





Misty

Washington State has a mist that covers the high trees and has an errie feel on certain days. The dark and grey has a cool and calm feel.

Mountains

The Cascade Mountains stretch from north California up until Canada. West of the cascades is the grey and dark side.
